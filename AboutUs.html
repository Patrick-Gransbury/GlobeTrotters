What is GlobeTrotters?

GlobeTrotters is an app that gives travellers the opportunity to find others with similar interests. We created GlobeTrotters to allow people to make new friends and explore the world. Our goal is to bring people of all backgrounds closer together.

Sign up today!

Why choose GlobeTrotters?

We give our users the ability to plan their perfect holiday. While there are "ride sharing" apps already out there, these are not designed to help people develop friendships. Rather than sharing rides, we are focused on sharing experiences and memories with new friends.

Sign up today!

How do I use GlobeTrotters?

Simply create an account and fill out your profile details. After you've finished setting up, you can search for potential travelling partners who share similar interests. Once you match with someone, you can start to plan out your perfect holiday together!

Safety

The safety of our users is incredibly important. That is why we have measures in place to ensure that you are safe and responsible when accessing and using GlobeTrotters.

  • We store your data securely by making use of encryption and hashing algorithms.
  • We utilise two-step verification to ensure that users are who they say they are.
  • We strongly encourage video calling people that you match with before meeting up.
  • We advise meeting up with people who you match with before going on holiday.
  • You must use GlobeTrotters responsibly. Please be careful and do not give out sensitive information to other users.
